Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

A bike helmet is not to be considered a one-time investment. Bike helmets have a shelf life and need to be replaced once in a while – even if they’re not involved in a crash.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Generally, a motorcycle can sit for about a month without someone riding it. However, any longer than that requires it to be properly prepared for storage.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

The most important rule of cycling is to wear a helmet, no matter how short your ride may be. Helmets protect your head in case of an accident and reduce the risk of brain injury.
